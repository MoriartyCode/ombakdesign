About

Ombak means wave.

We liked that as a name. Good work moves steadily — it shapes things over time and tends to leave the surface calmer than it found it.

Ombak is a small studio, which means you work directly with the people doing the work. No layers, no hand-offs to a junior you never met. Just considered branding and product design, shipped.
